PREPARATION

- A husky should have his own bed, a water dish and a food bowl
- Introduce your husky to his bedding area when you bring him home
- Provide your dog with plenty of dog toys to keep him from chewing on other objects

TRAINING AND EXERCISE

- Enroll yourself and your Siberian husky in obedience classes
- Take your husky for 30-minute walks at least once a day to help work off their excess energy
- Give your husky plenty of time to run and play outside

DIET AND FOOD

- Feed your Siberian husky high-quality dry dog food
- Keep your husky's water bowl full and refresh the water at least once a day
- Take your husky to the vet regularly for checkups and vaccinations

GROOMING

- Brush your husky's coat once a week using a wide-toothed comb or large bristle brush
- Bathe your husky only when necessary, which should be infrequently
- Clean your husky's ears once a week using a dog ear cleansing solution
