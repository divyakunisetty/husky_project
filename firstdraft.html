HUSKIES

Types of Huskies   Husky Quiz   Caring for your Husky

Husky is a general name for a sled-type of dog used in northern regions, differentiated from other sled-dog types
by their fast pulling style.They are an ever-changing cross-breed of the fastest dogs. In recent years, companies have been
marketing tourist treks with dog sledges for adventure travellers in snow regions as well. Huskies are also today kept as
pets,and groups work to find new pet homes for retired racing and adventure trekking dogs.
